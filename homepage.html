A little bit about me...
I'm a Junior at the Ira A. Fulton School of Engineering at Arizona State University studying Computer Science alongside a Game Development Certificate. I never really knew what I wanted to do when I got to college but decided that Computer Science seemed interesting so I went with that. That decision was one of the best decisions I could have made as I have absolutely loved everything there was to learn about Computer Science. The projects you can create are endless and that excites me the most about programming and I look forward to what I learn next.
